About

I'm drawn to the space where marketing, technology, and human behavior meet. Projects

From Prompt to Production

Teaching marketers to build technology rather than simply use it.

In the Vibe Coding: From Prompt to Portfolio workshop within MarTech Studio, students construct a lightweight marketing technology stack using Visual Studio Code, GitHub Copilot, Git, and GitHub Pages. Through this process, students learn how AI-assisted coding environments allow marketers to design and deploy digital experiences without traditional engineering backgrounds.

Using this stack, students build and deploy their own portfolio websites, moving from prompt to production while learning the fundamentals of version control, deployment workflows, and AI-assisted development.

The goal is not to turn marketers into software engineers, but to help them understand how modern marketing systems are built and how AI tools expand their ability to prototype and test ideas quickly.

MarTech Stack Design

Building frameworks that help organizations understand how technologies interact across the customer lifecycle. My work integrates customer intelligence, personalization engines, analytics platforms, and engagement tools into cohesive systems—enabling marketers to move beyond individual tools toward scalable marketing ecosystems.

Data Storytelling and Strategic Insight

Helping students and organizations translate complex datasets into strategic narratives through visualization, analysis, and frameworks like the Ansoff matrix. Students learn to move beyond describing data toward identifying opportunities, supporting recommendations, and recognizing what may be missing.

Experiential Campaign Management

Student teams design and launch live email campaigns using CRM and marketing automation platforms, then track opens, clickthroughs, and conversions in real time. These projects transform theoretical concepts into measurable outcomes, building confidence to operate in real marketing environments.
